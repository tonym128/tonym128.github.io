Hi, I'm Tony Mamacos. Welcome to **ttech - Technology Adventures**.

I'm an avid fan of technology in both my personal and professional life. I like to explore the latest and greatest tech, preferably in the Open Source space. 

This blog is a collection of my adventures in new, upcoming, and unexplored technologies. Some of the areas I frequently dive into include:

*   **Software Engineering & AI:** Exploring the boundaries of what's possible, from creative prompting and building AI-assisted tools to diving into large language models and procedural generation.
*   **Game Development & Graphics:** Tinkering with WebGL, shaders, real-time rendering, and building high-speed polygonal games or tools like procedural texture generators.
*   **Hardware & Tinkering:** Getting hands-on with microcontrollers (ESP32, Raspberry Pi), building custom game consoles, soldering badges, and whatever other hardware projects catch my eye.
*   **Cybersecurity:** Occasional write-ups on my experiences at events like DEF CON.

Feel free to browse around, check out my projects, and explore the code on my [GitHub](https://github.com/tonym128).

If you'd like to get in touch, you can reach me at [tmamacos@gmail.com](mailto:tmamacos@gmail.com).
